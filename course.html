About US

TIKRAPARA A.M. HIGH SCHOOL was established in 1949 and it is managed by the Department of Education. It is located in Rural area. It is located in PATASHPUR -II block of PURBA MEDINIPUR district of West Bengal. The school consists of Grades from 5 to 12.
